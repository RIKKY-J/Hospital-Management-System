About City Hospital

City Hospital is a trusted healthcare institution dedicated to delivering compassionate, patient-centered medical care. Established in 1995, we have proudly served our community for over two decades with a commitment to excellence, innovation, and accessibility for every individual in need of healthcare services.

Our Mission

To provide world-class healthcare services that are affordable, accessible, and delivered with empathy and integrity. We aim to improve the quality of life in our community through innovation, modern medical practices, and a team of dedicated professionals.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leading healthcare provider in the region — offering advanced technology, outstanding patient experience, and an unwavering commitment to care and cure.

Our Services

Emergency Care

24/7 emergency services with skilled doctors and rapid response teams.

Advanced Surgery

Modern surgical facilities and expert surgeons ensuring safe, precise operations.

Diagnostics

High-quality imaging and lab testing for accurate, timely diagnoses.

Specialized Departments

Cardiology, Orthopedics, Pediatrics, and more — specialized care for every need.
